Intoduction

FOAMTREAT®HP high-performance defoamer is a highly effective silicone defoamer for use in water base drilling fluid, completion fluid and workover fluid, especially in reservoir drill-in fluid. The defoamer can be readily added to water-base fluids to prevent or control foaming, particularly when surfactant additives are used. The defoamer is compatible with brine systems and fluid-loss-control pills. FOAMTREAT®HP deformer has minimal impact on fluid rheology and performs well in both monovalent and divalent brines.

Applications/Functions

  • Used in water base drilling fluid, completion fluid and workover fluid

  • Compatible with brine systems and fluid-loss-control pills

Advantages

  • Minimal impact on fluid rheology

  • Non-toxicity product and environmentally acceptable

Typical properties

  • Appearance: White liquid

  • Specific gravity: 1.0

Recommended Treatment

  • A typical treatment range is 0.05-0.2 ppb
