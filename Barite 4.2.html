Intoduction

BARITE 4.2 weighting material is a high-quality, drilling-grade barite used to increase the density of drilling fluids. This high-specific-gravity mineral is the most widely used weight material, has application in all drilling fluid systems and meets both API 13A section 7 specifications and GB/T 5005-2010- Grade I for barite. BARITE 4.2 can be used to increase the density in oil-and water-based drilling fluids up to 21ppg (2.52SG). It is chemical inert and does not affect drilling fluid chemical properties.

Applications/Functions

  • Increases drilling fluids density up to 21ppg (2.52SG)

  • Control underground kicks and lost circulation with lost circulation with barite slugs

Advantages

  • Standard weighting material for drilling fluid

  • Essentially chemically inert and insoluble, functions only in a physical manner

  • Does not react with other drilling fluid additives or interfere with their function

  • Minimally abrasive

Typical properties

  • Appearance: Gray Powder

  • Specific gravity: ≥ 4.2

  • Soluble alkaline Earth Metals (as calcium), mg/kg: ≤250

  • Particle size >75μm (wet screen), %wt: ≤3

  • Particle size <6μm (sedimentation), %wt: ≤30
