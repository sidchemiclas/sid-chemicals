Intoduction

FOAMTREAT®AK additive is a blend of high-molecular-weight alcohol and modified derivatives defoaming agent formulated with higher esters to control foaming in all water-base drilling fluids containing freshwater, brackish water, seawater, and saturated saltwater, as well as completion fluids.

Applications/Functions

  • Low concentration treatment with highly effective
