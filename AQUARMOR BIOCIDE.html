Intoduction

AQUARMOR® is a concentrated thiocarbamate-based biocide used in water-base drilling, completion, workover and packer fluids. It is an effective biocide at low concentration levels. AQUARMOR®can be used in any water-base mud to control the growth of bacteria and fungus. It can be used with oxygen scavenger, but is not compatible with sulphite and bisulfite-based scavengers.

Applications/Functions

  • Used in water-based drilling fluids to control bacteria

  • Also suitable in completion/workover and packer fluids

  • Used as waste water treatment

Advantages

  • Effective against a broad range of microbes, bacteria and fungi

  • Compatible with most water-based drilling fluids

  • Flash point:＞212°F

Recommended Treatment
