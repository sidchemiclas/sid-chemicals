Intoduction

EZWASH® is a blend of selected anionic and non-ionic surfactants with variety of uses ranging from general rig and engine cleanup to specialized cuttings wash solutions. EZWASH® helps reduce the potential for bit balling, helps improve ROP and helps reduce the stickiness of water sensitive cuttings. EZWASH® is a heavy duty, low-sudsing detergent and degreaser, which makes those tough cleanup jobs easier.

Applications/Functions

  • Increase ROP by improving bit cleaning and release of cuttings from the bit surface

  • Retard breakup of cuttings in annulus

  • Promote settling of cuttings at the surface

  • Counteract the sticking tendencies of clays and thereby reduce wall-packing, bit balling, booting-off, and formation mud rings

Advantages

  • Very low concentration with improved ROP performance-0.1% V/V

  • Reduce the potential for bit balling

  • Environmental acceptable product and readily biodegradable

Typical properties

  • Appearance: Pale red liquid

  • Flash Point:>149°F

  • Specific gravity: 1.05

  • pH (10% in water): 9.5

Recommended Treatment

  • Typically add 0.25-1 ppb to help promote bit cleaning and reduce stickiness.
