Intoduction

EZTHIN® is an environmental acceptable high performance thinner used in water-based drilling fluids up to 400°F. It is modified tannin compound that replaces chrome lignosulfonates. EZTHIN® is 4-8 times more effective than chrome lignosulfonates. It requires no caustic to activate as solution pH is approximately 8.5. EZTHIN® can provide maximum deflocculation and shear strength reduction at low concentrations. EZTHIN® is widely used in high salinity, high hardness, gypsum, any pH and recognized as the most cost-effective thinners for WBM.

Applications/Functions

  • Reduces gel strength and rheology properties in both dispersed and non-dispersed WBM while maintaining hole cleaning capabilities

  • Functions in fresh water, sea water, salt water and divalent brine systems

  • Can effectively treat contamination from salt, cement, anhydrite, drilled solids

Advantages

  • Temperatures stable up to 400°

  • Excellent performance at low concentrations

  • Tolerant to high salinity, high hardness, gypsum and any pH

  • Contains no chrome

Typical properties

  • Appearance: Reddish brown powder

  • Specific gravity: 0.47

  • pH: 6

Recommended Treatment

  • A typical range is 1- 4 ppb, but specific drilling conditions will dictate a suitable concentration
