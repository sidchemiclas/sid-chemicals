Intoduction

EZKLEAN® casing cleaner is used during the displacement of oil-based and water-based drilling fluids. Typically, EZKLEAN® casing cleaner is used for surface cleaning operation and as the final pill in a displacement squeeze. Its function is to emulsify residual oil, remove polar and leave surface water wet.

Applications/Functions

  • Casing cleaner

  • Remove oil-based drilling fluids residues from surface

Advantages

  • Improved casing wash performance helps reduce clean up time

  • Environmental friendly product

Typical properties

  • Appearance: Clear Liquid

  • Specific gravity: 1.0

  • pH (10% in water): 7

Recommended Treatment

  • A typical treatment range is 20% by volume in brine or seawater
