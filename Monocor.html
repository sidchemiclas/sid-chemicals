Intoduction

MONOCOR®corrosion inhibitor is a water-soluble filming agent containing monoethanolamine. It is effective in drilling fluids, completion fluids and brine packer fluids. It can be used in a range of monovalent and divalent brines. MONOCOR® provides protection for drill pipe, casing and other tubulars in drilling and completion systems. Corrosion protection can be maintained at temperatures up to 375°F.

Applications/Functions

  • Water-base drilling fluids, completion fluids

  • Brine packer fluids

Advantages

  • Easily dispersed in water

  • High temperature corrosion control

  • Effective at low concentrations

  • Environmental acceptable product

Typical properties

  • Appearance: Clear liquid;

  • Flash point: 205°F

  • Specific gravity: 1.02

  • pH (1% aqueous solution): 12

Recommended Treatment

  • Corrosion can be controlled and limited with the addition of up to 0.4% v/v MONOCOR®

  • Additions to divalent brines should be limited in order to control precipitation
